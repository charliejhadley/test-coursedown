Part C of the assessment requires you to use RMarkdown to analyse and visualise a survey dataset. The data comes from the paper, “The importance of urban natural areas and urban ecosystem services during the COVID-19 pandemic” by Nelson Grima et al. You can find all assets from this paper in this Figshare collection. Please do read the paper in preparation to answer the assessment questions in Part C.

  • The dataset itself is available from this DOI - doi.org/10.1371/journal.pone.0243344.s001.

  • The survey questions can be found here - doi.org/10.1371/journal.pone.0243344.s002
